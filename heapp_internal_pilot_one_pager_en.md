# Heapp Internal Pilot One-Pager (20% Allocation)

## Business Goal
Increase product decision speed and quality by turning player/support signals into measurable product actions, while keeping support KPI stable.

## Scope (20% Time)
- Allocation: 1 day/week (or equivalent split).
- Duration: 8 weeks pilot.
- Role mode: Customer Success Lead + Product Analyst (transition track).

## Inputs Required
- Weekly export of top support ticket tags and volumes.
- Discord/Steam feedback snapshots or summary exports.
- Access to release notes and known-issues list.
- One product owner contact for weekly alignment.

## Outputs
1. Weekly Insight Memo (1 page): top issues, hypotheses, proposed experiments.
2. Prioritized Backlog Slice: P0/P1/P2 with effort/risk/KPI mapping.
3. Monthly Product Signal Report: trends, regressions, and decision recommendations.

## KPI Framework
- **Primary:**
  - Time from signal to decision (days).
  - Number of decisions supported by structured analysis.
- **Product:**
  - D1/D7 movement on pilot-related issues (where measurable).
  - Invite Success Rate / Quit<10m / session continuity proxies.
- **Support Guardrails:**
  - No drop in support SLA.
  - No increase in unresolved critical queue.

## Guardrails
- Support KPI must remain at or above baseline.
- Pilot tasks must be limited to high-impact and measurable items.
- Weekly review with lead to re-scope if support pressure spikes.

## Timeline
- Week 1: Baseline + initial signal map.
- Week 2-3: First recommendation batch and quick-win experiments.
- Week 4: Mid-pilot review and KPI checkpoint.
- Week 5-7: Second batch and cross-functional alignment.
- Week 8: Pilot readout + transition recommendation.

## Risks and Mitigation
- **Risk:** No direct DB access.  
  **Mitigation:** start with support/community data + request minimum aggregated exports.
- **Risk:** Support workload spike.  
  **Mitigation:** fixed pilot time block, fallback to high-priority-only cadence.
- **Risk:** No measurable outcome in cycle 1.  
  **Mitigation:** narrow to one short-loop KPI (for example, TTR or invite flow).

## Success Criteria
- At least 2 pilot deliverables accepted by product/dev stakeholders.
- At least 1 team decision made using pilot analysis.
- Written agreement on next step: expanded pilot or formal role transition track.
